# Introduction

Prepwise is a next-generation mock interview and employability enhancement platform tailored for fresh IT graduates. Designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations, the platform simulates real-world interview environments and provides personalized feedback to help candidates improve their skills. Leveraging Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and GAN-based models, Prepwise aims to make job preparation more intelligent, data-driven, and outcome-oriented.

## Key Features

- **Resume Analysis**: Automatically extracts data from resumes to tailor mock interview questions and job recommendations.

- **AI-Powered Interview Feedback**: Uses LLMs and NLP techniques to assess grammar, tone, and confidence levels in candidate responses.

- **Confidence Level Detection**: Analyzes video/audio responses to estimate confidence and communication efficiency.

- **Feedback Generation**: Offers detailed suggestions for improvement, grammar corrections, and tips based on candidate performance.

- **Job Recommendation System**: Utilizes GANs to match candidates with suitable job roles based on skills, resume data, and interview outcomes.

## Technical Insights

- **Smart Contracts**: Developed using Solidity to handle voting logic and ensure tamper-proof results.
- **Frontend**: Built with React and Web3.js for seamless interaction with the blockchain.
- **Storage**: Used IPFS for storing voter data securely and efficiently.
- **Security**: Implemented multi-signature wallets for election administrators to prevent unauthorized access.

## Outcome

Prepwise successfully demonstrates how AI and modern web technologies can reshape interview preparation and skill development. Through continuous evaluation and feedback, the platform empowers candidates to refine their soft skills, technical responses, and confidence. The system was evaluated with test candidates and showed promising improvements in performance and self-awareness.
